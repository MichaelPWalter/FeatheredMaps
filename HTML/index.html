Common Kingfisher
LC
Alcedo atthis

Uncommon, seasonal migrant


A small, vibrant bird with a striking electric-blue back and orange underparts. It has a large head, a long, dagger-like bill, and short tail. Often seen perched motionless near water or darting low over the surface in a flash of blue.


Found along slow-moving rivers, streams, lakes, and ponds with clear water and abundant small fish, often in areas with overhanging vegetation for perching.
